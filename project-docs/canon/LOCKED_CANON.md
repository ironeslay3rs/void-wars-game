\# LOCKED CANON



These truths must NEVER change or drift.



\## Core World Truths

\- The Void is a 3D prison.

\- Humanity exists inside the Void as a diminished form.

\- Evolution is the only path forward.



\## Evolution Paths

\- Bio = Verdant Coil (body, mutation, adaptation)

\- Mecha = Chrome Synod (mind, precision, control)

\- Pure = Ember Vault (soul, memory, fire)



\## Fusion Truth

\- True evolution = body + mind + soul

\- Fusion is NOT available in Book 1



\## Black Market

\- Neutral survivor citadel

\- Place of trade, risk, and forbidden power



\## Tone

\- Survival-first

\- Costly progression

\- No heroic fantasy tone

\- Everything has weight and consequence

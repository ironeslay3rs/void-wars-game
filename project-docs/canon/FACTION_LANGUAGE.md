\# FACTION LANGUAGE



\## Allowed Terms

\- Bio (Verdant Coil)

\- Mecha (Chrome Synod)

\- Pure (Ember Vault)



\## Forbidden Terms

\- "Spirit" (do NOT use in UI)

\- Generic RPG naming like "mage", "warrior"



\## Tone per faction



\### Bio

\- Organic

\- Mutated

\- Predatory

\- Alive



\### Mecha

\- Engineered

\- Precise

\- Cold

\- Controlled



\### Pure

\- Calm

\- Memory-based

\- Fire and soul

\- Human-centered



\## Rule

All UI text must match faction tone.

# Void Wars: Oblivion Canon Registry

## Purpose
This file is the source-of-truth registry for repo-facing narrative canon. Use it to align features, UI text, content design, progression systems, and future docs.

## Locked Canon

### World Law
- The world is the Void, a 3D prison.
- Humanity is the 3D shadow or remnant of higher-dimensional humanity.
- Gods, dragons, and mythic beings were cast into the Void.
- Evolution is the singular force that could not truly be contained because its power is adaptability, not brute force.
- The true final path is Fusion: body + mind + soul.

### Black Market Law
- The Black Market is a neutral survivor citadel between the three empires.
- Deals are sacred; betrayal costs more than death.

### Power Law
- Rune levels are 1 through 7.
- Saint Runes are Level 5.
- Level 7 cannot be forged inside the Void.
- Rune Knights and Rune-Smiths are core systems.

### Character Spine
- Iron is the central character spine.
- Richard is a key rune-smith mentor figure.
- Erick is tied to the Black Market.
- Pride and Sloth are major rulers.
- The sisters, rivals, Blood Curse / Lineage Plunder, and Rune Knights are core long-term story and game pillars.

### Game Thesis
- The game is a war-scarred cultivation expedition RPG.
- Core loop: prepare in the citadel -> endure the front -> extract with scars and salvage -> forge what you need to return -> contribute to the war -> attempt breakthrough.
- Survival, preparation, crafting necessity, faction war, and ascension-through-consequence come first.
- Reject generic gacha clutter, too many currencies, too many tabs, and reward-first UI.

## Flexible Implementation Space
These items may be designed, tuned, or staged without changing canon if they preserve the locked rules above.

- Exact faction mechanics, bonuses, and progression pacing.
- Mission structures, run lengths, and encounter composition.
- Resource names, counts, and crafting recipes.
- UI architecture, information hierarchy, and onboarding flows.
- The order in which nations, rulers, lanes, or character arcs become playable or visible.
- Placeholder content that clearly avoids contradicting locked canon.

## Usage Rules for Future Tasks
- Treat every item under Locked Canon as non-negotiable unless the user explicitly revises canon.
- Separate canon facts from implementation choices in specs, tickets, and content docs.
- When a feature needs undefined lore, add an assumption note instead of inventing permanent canon.
- If current game content contradicts this registry, preserve scope and log the conflict before changing game systems.
